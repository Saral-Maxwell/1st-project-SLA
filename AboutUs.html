Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science, formerly Hindustan collage of Engieering, is a private deemed university headquartered in chennai, India. It was founded in 1985 by K.C.G Vergese and was conferred the "University Status". Facilities HITS offers top-notch facilities for students, including tech-savvy classrooms, dining facilities, sports amenities, libraries, transportation services, banking and ATM facilities, auditoriums, and a green campus. Academics HITS offers a range of specialized schools and programs with cutting-edge resources and expert faculties. The university emphasizes corporate values and skills for complex decision-making, as well as developing expertise in functional domains and business knowledge.
